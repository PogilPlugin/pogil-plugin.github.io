Welcome to the Microsoft Word POGIL Plugin

The POGIL (Process Oriented Guided Inquiry Learning) Plugin for Microsoft Word enables educators to create, manage, and share collaborative learning activities seamlessly.

Key Features:

  • Create guided inquiry learning activities directly in Word.
  • Custom templates for students and instructors.
  • Export documents as PDFs for sharing.
  • Integrated feedback and progress tracking tools.

Getting Started:

  1. Select a section from the navigation menu on the left.
  2. Follow the guided steps to create or edit an activity.
  3. Save and export your document for distribution.
